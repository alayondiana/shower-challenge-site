How it works

Four steps. One shorter shower.

No sign-up, no setup — just open the app when you step in.

Step 1

Select a challenge

Pick a way to shorten your shower that fits your routine.

Step 2

Start the timer

Tap start the moment you step in and the water turns on.

Step 3

Stop the timer

Tap stop as soon as you're done and the water's off.

Step 4

See how you did

Track your progress, build a streak, and beat your best time.
